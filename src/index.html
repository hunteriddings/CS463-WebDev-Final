About

Hello! My name is Hunter Iddings. This is my final project for CS463 (Intro to Web Development) at Portland State University , and contains my experience, 4 recent projects, and a contact form to reach out to me.

I have over five years of web development experience, working with technologies like HTML, SCSS, JavaScript, and Node.js. Beyond technology, I also have experience in the packaging industry, specializing in supply chain management, territory management, marketing, and international sales.

Website: ProSource Bag

ProSource Bag is a flexible packaging company specializing in high-quality packaging solutions, including rice bags, animal feed poly bags, child-resistant bags, and stand-up pouches. The company serves brands, distributors, and mills, providing durable and customizable packaging options.

As a trusted packaging partner, ProSource serves companies like SunRice (FRA: $7H0), Sun Valley Rice, Wismettac Asian Foods, and many more, providing innovative and highly specialized sustainable packaging solutions tailored to their needs.

Website: BioShell Tableware

BioShell Tableware is a subsidiary of ProSource Bag, specializing in manufacturing high-quality compostable food service ware. Using sustainable materials such as fiber bagasse, PLA, and paper, BioShell provides eco-friendly alternatives to traditional plastic packaging for restaurants, caterers, and food service businesses.

As part of its commitment to environmental sustainability, BioShell Tableware supports the Waste Management Phoenix Open (WMPO) by supplying compostable packaging, helping the event maintain its Zero Waste status. By reducing landfill waste and promoting sustainable practices, BioShell plays a key role in one of the greenest sporting events in the world.

Website: TechStart

TechStart is a nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing computer science education in Oregon. By providing resources, mentorship, and opportunities for students and educators, TechStart helps bridge the gap between education and industry. The organization supports K-12 schools with funding, curriculum development, and hands-on learning experiences such as coding competitions and hackathons.

As a volunteer, I contribute to TechStart’s mission by helping students develop their technical skills, actively maining social media accounts and website, and promoting access to technology education across the state. Through these efforts, TechStart empowers the next generation of innovators and strengthens Oregon’s tech community.

Article: Lake Oswego School District Sustainability Initiative

As a Business Director at BioShell Tableware, I spearheaded a partnership with the Lake Oswego School District to introduce biodegradable wood sporks into the district's elementary school lunch programs. This initiative aimed to replace plastic utensils with more sustainable, eco-friendly alternatives, aligning with the district's sustainability goals. The project not only provides a greener solution but also educates young students about the importance of reducing plastic waste.
